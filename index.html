Who we are ?

Ashwin Enterpises Pvt Ltd is a trusted overseas manpower recruitment agency in India.

With a legacy of more than 25 years we specialize in recruitment, staffing and training across various industries such as Constrution, Manufacturing, oil & Gas, Security Services, Health & hospitality sectors. Job Evaluation

According to your job requirements, we assess the qualifications, skills and experience required for the applicants. Based on that we do a thorough screening and shortlisting of the candidates.

We arrange the medical screening, certificate attestation, visa stamping, ticketing and emigration clearance of the selected candidates ensuring a smooth timely deployment to your site.

Placement Services
