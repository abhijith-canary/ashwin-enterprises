Who we are ?

Ashwin Enterpises Pvt Ltd is a trusted overseas manpower recruitment agency in India.

We are approved and licensed by the Ministry od External Affairs, Government od India. With a legacy of more than 25 years we specialize in recruitment, staffing and training across various industries such as Constrution, Manufacturing, oil & Gas, Security Services, Health & hospitality sectors. Our services include providing professionals, managers, skilled technicians, security guards, labourers etc. tailored to our clients' needs. Interviews and trade tests are organised at multiple centers across the country depending upon the categories and numbers that are required. We prioritize long-term partnerships and offer comprehensive solutions for the organizational success of our clients.
